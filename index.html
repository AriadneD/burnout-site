How It Works

1. Data Collection & Privacy

We protect the privacy of your device data by leveraging proprietary encryption and secure data collection methods. When you use our platform, any information is not accessible to individual employers or specific people other than yourself.

2. Algorithmic Analysis

Based on behavioral data collected from your devices, our advanced AI and digital phenotyping algorithms focus on patterns and variations in daily activities such as phone usage, physical movements, and sleep quality, identifying deviations indicative of stress or burnout.

3. Insights & Resources

Our recommendations algorithm uses machine learning to provide personalized insights and resources on where, when, and how to improve your well-being, performance, and mental health. You can view your progress over time, building resilience and enhancing your ability to manage workplace stress.
